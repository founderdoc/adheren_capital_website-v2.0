Meaning Behind Our Name & Logo

In cell biology adheren junctions are protein complexes that form the junction between cells. They can fully encapsulate a cell or just form a specific spot attachment of two cells. These important cell to cell junctions serve a dual purpose, they maintain tissue architecture and facilitate cell movement during tissue development and renewal (2010 Nature article). Similar to the function in biology, we are a junction that forms connections between companies in order to facilitate growth, development, and renewal. Through the constellation of these junctions that surround a company or connect only a portion of a company we help enable greater functional possibilities that could only be achieved through realizing these connections.

The butterfly in our logo represents transformation into a beautiful admired entity. Like the wings on a Morpho Butterfly, we develop unique structures that specifically reinforce wavelengths forming beautiful light while canceling out other undesirable wavelengths. Each scale that enables the beautiful color of the wings to be created is like a pixel that wehn combined creates impactful images that connect us.


Every single company we work with, each of their employees, and the people that make up our team operate as independent pixels that when combined make a marvelous entity.


Nature creates miraculous possibilities that stimulate our imagination. At Adheren Capital we help make possibilities of the imagination a natural reality.

Our Investment Criteria

We look for stable traditional businesses that have at least $1.5M EBITDA with a proven profit generating track record to whom we can bring compelling market advantage.

Ancillary Innovators

Ancillary Innovators are not in the spotlight but play a crucial role in augmenting and enhancing the value of the core offering. Operating 2 or 3 degrees away from the central product, they exhibit adaptability, innovation, and a robust understanding of industry nuances.

Consistent Profitability

We value enterprises that demonstrate a steady financial performance over time and showing resilience against market fluctuations. This financial steadiness allows for a strategic focus on long-term objectives, paving the way for sustained success and mutual prosperity.

Vibrant Corporate Culture

A thriving corporate culture holds significant weight. We seek enterprises where positivity, support, and encouragement permeate the work environment, cultivating a sense of belonging and mutual growth.

Dynamic Leadership

Leadership depth is a critical aspect of the enterprises we consider for investment. We look for businesses led by forward-thinking individuals possessing the knowledge and capability to ascend into more senior roles as necessitated by evolving business demands.
